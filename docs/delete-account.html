Request Account and Data Deletion

If you would like your Pomoductive account and saved session history deleted, you can request deletion by email.

How to request deletion

  1. Email us from the account associated with Pomoductive.
  2. Use the subject line: Pomoductive Account Deletion Request
  3. Include your Pomoductive username in the email so we can identify your account.
Request by Email

What will be deleted

  • Your Pomoductive account login.
  • Your saved Pomodoro session history and statistics.

What may be retained

  • Limited records may be retained temporarily if required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.
We will process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe after verifying the request.
